Lasagna di mama

Very easy and delicious recipe

Ingridients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Mince fish
  • Chopped Tomatos
  • Onion
  • Secret Spices
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Glorious Cheese

Steps

  1. prepare magic red sauce
  2. Stack magic sauce with lasagna sheets and bechamel sauce
  3. repeat until run out
  4. Grate glorious cheese on top
  5. shove in oven for 45 mins at 180c fan, search google for other ovens
  6. Enjoy!!
